# Robotic Invariance Audits

Submission-hardening v2 artifact for paper 58 in the robotics 60-paper batch.

## Decision

Workshop-only.

The paper is useful as a falsification and reporting protocol for embodied invariance claims, but it is not submission-ready as a robot-learning result. The evidence is a deterministic synthetic diagnostic plus a local literature sweep. V2 hardening also shows that the original generated `stagewise_audit` condition cannot be claimed as a policy improvement.

## V2 hardening result

The same-policy measurement control treats the audit as an observer on the aggregate-robustness policy. Policy success stays fixed at 0.614. At a 0.12 invariance-gap threshold, the observer exposes 23 of 28 collapsed transform-stage cells, including two outcome-passing hidden transformations: `camera_yaw` and `lighting_texture`.
